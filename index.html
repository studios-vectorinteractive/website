Where Experience Exceeds Expectations

About Us

Here at VectorInteractive Studios, we develop games which are based on the Indian subcontinent. Our motto is "Where Experience exceeds Expectations". Whether it is the rich and beautifully diverse history of the ancient India, or the stories about the valor of our Indian armed forces which we have witnessed ourselves, or an open world of the beautiful vallies of our Northeastern India. We plan to develop games in India, by Indian developers and by an Indian company. The games which have pure Indian feel to it. Our mission is to develop games that will make our Indian audience feel connected to their culture. For our non-Indian cunsumers, our games will bring them a whole new dimension to their experience, a whole new world to explore and interact with. We are recreating an unexplored world to be explored by you.

Our First Game

We are very happy to announce that we will deploy our very first game, The Daunting House, on the two platforms, steam and on Play Store, in the last week of September. The game is set in the beautiful vallyes of the mountains in the Notheast India. Jack, the character, came to these valleys on a trek with his friends, but soon he finds out, while exploring the mesmerising beauty of the valley, none of his friends was with him. Jack was now lost. He runs in the dense forest, here and there, searching for his friends. He realises that the sun is going down and his friends are still nowhere to find. At a little distance, he sees a house, a house that's very famous by the name "The Daunting House.". It's the house, which no one has ever managed to escape. He stops and thinks for a while, but in the end, he decides to go and look for his friends near the periphery of the house. The Sun has now disappeared and the game has now begun.
